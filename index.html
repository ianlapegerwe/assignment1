Ians Favorites!
My name is Ian Lape-Gerwe. I was born in suburban N. Kentucky and attended catholic grade school and high school. I first discovered art in grade school, when I attended Baker Hunt for classes. I knew it was something I wanted to pursue when I took classes in high school with Tim Haders. My art reflects my need to break away from my conservative, suburban, Catholic upbringing. I like to explore taboo imaging and different cultures. My art also reflects my love of music. I appreciate bands in the genre of The Grateful Dead and you will see that influence in many of my pieces. My favorite medium is ceramics because I like working with my hands and respect the forgiving sculptural element of clay.
